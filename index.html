Pixelators

Empowering the Next Generation

 

We are dedicated to imparting knowledge in the fields of STEM to the next generation.

 

As a non-profit and educational outreach organization, Pixelators aims to grant universal access to knowledge in all STEM fields. We believe that young individuals are the future of the world and deserve optimal education. Therefore, Pixelators has created myriad free classes with superb teaching, a solid syllabus, and most importantly, a good foundation. 

Both our online and physical presence utilize modern teaching techniques and an effective course plan. Our main goal is to impart proper education to all individuals who want, crave, or need it. We constantly update our material in order to stay up to date with the latest material. 

The dynamic and growing fields of STEM are becoming increasingly common in our everyday lives, and Pixelators believes knowledge in these fields are essential. Education is invaluable, and Pixelators has a mission to make it accessible to everyone. 

-A message from the Pixelators Team
